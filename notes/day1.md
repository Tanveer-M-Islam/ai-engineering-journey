What is AI Engineering?

AI Engineering is the process of building real-world applications using AI models like GPT, Claude, Gemini.

Difference between ML Engineer and AI Engineer:

ML Engineer:
- Focus on training models
- Works with datasets
- Uses deep learning

AI Engineer:
- Uses APIs
- Builds applications
- Focus on product

Why AI Engineering is growing:
- Companies want fast solutions
- APIs make AI easy
- Demand is very high
